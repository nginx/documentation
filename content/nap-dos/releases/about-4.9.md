F5 DoS for NGINX provides behavioral protection against Denial of Service (DoS) attacks for your web applications.

## Release 4.9

December 1, 2025

### New features

- NGINX Plus R37 support
- Debian 13 (Trixie) support
- RHEL 10 and Rocky Linux 10 support
- Bug fixes
