## About

F5 Kitchen Brew Console makes it easy to manage connected brewers across locations and environments. The console lets you monitor and control your brewer fleet from one place — you can check extraction targets, track maintenance schedules, identify firmware that needs updating, manage user roles, and more. Brew Console is part of F5 Kitchen, which includes [F5 Kitchen components](#f5-kitchen-components).
